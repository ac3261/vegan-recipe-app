# Recipe Nutrition Summary

The nutrition summary provides a breakdown of the nutritional content of your recipe. This includes calories, macronutrients, and micronutrients.

## Macronutrients

* Calories: The total energy content of the recipe.
* Protein: The amount of protein in the recipe.
* Carbohydrates: The amount of carbohydrates in the recipe.
* Total Fat: The total amount of fat in the recipe.
* Saturated Fat: The amount of saturated fat in the recipe.
* Total Sugars: The total amount of sugars in the recipe.
* Fiber: The amount of fiber in the recipe.

## Micronutrients

* Micronutrients: A list of micronutrients present in the recipe.

## How to Read the Nutrition Summary

The nutrition summary is displayed in a table format, making it easy to compare the nutritional content of different recipes. Each row represents a different nutrient, and the values are displayed in a clear and concise manner.
