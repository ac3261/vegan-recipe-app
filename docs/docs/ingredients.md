## Ingredients

Ingredients are the building blocks of any recipe. They can be combined in various ways to create delicious dishes.

### How to Add Ingredients

To add ingredients to your recipe, simply type the name of the ingredient in the search bar. You can also select from a list of pre-populated ingredients.

### Ingredient Options

The following ingredients are available:

* Proteins (chicken, beef, pork, tofu, etc.)
* Vegetables (carrots, broccoli, bell peppers, etc.)
* Fruits (apples, bananas, berries, etc.)
* Grains (rice, quinoa, bread, etc.)
* Dairy (milk, cheese, yogurt, etc.)
* Nuts and seeds (almonds, walnuts, chia seeds, etc.)

### Custom Ingredients

You can also add custom ingredients to your recipe. Simply type the name of the ingredient in the search bar and select it from the list of options.

### Removing Ingredients

To remove an ingredient from your recipe, simply click on the 'Remove' button next to the ingredient name.
