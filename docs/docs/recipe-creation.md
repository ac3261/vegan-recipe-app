# Creating a Recipe

When creating a recipe, you can include a nutrition summary to provide a breakdown of the nutritional content of your recipe. This includes calories, macronutrients, and micronutrients.

## Including Nutrition Information

To include nutrition information in your recipe, make sure to provide the following details:

* Calories
* Protein
* Carbohydrates
* Total Fat
* Saturated Fat
* Total Sugars
* Fiber
* Micronutrients

You can include this information in the recipe by following the guidelines outlined in the [Recipe Nutrition Summary](recipe.md) section.
