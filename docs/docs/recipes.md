## Seasonings

Seasonings are a crucial part of any recipe. They add flavor and depth to your dishes.

### How to Add Seasonings

To add seasonings to your recipe, simply type the name of the seasoning in the search bar. You can also select from a list of pre-populated seasonings.

### Seasoning Options

The following seasonings are available:

* Salt
* Pepper
* Garlic powder
* Onion powder
* Paprika
* Cumin
* Coriander
* Turmeric
* Ginger
* Chili powder
* Oregano
* Thyme
* Basil

### Custom Seasonings

You can also add custom seasonings to your recipe. Simply type the name of the seasoning in the search bar and select it from the list of options.

### Removing Seasonings

To remove a seasoning from your recipe, simply click on the 'Remove' button next to the seasoning name.
